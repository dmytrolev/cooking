--- title: Узвар --- The Ukraine is known for its beautiful and abundant orchards. Fruit in many forms is eaten fresh and used cooked in dishes. Dried fruit is used year round. This compote, a traditional dish made from dried fruit, included dried legumes in olden times. In some regions, such as Bukovyna, the combination of dried prunes and beans still lives on. This recipe is a standard, widely used nineteenth~century version. page 23
